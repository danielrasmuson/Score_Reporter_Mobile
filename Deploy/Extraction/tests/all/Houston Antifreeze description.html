Rice University Women's Ultimate (Torque) is excited to announce the 4th annual Houston Antifreeze Tournament! It's a great way to start off the year with lots of playing time and touches on the disc.

Houston Antifreeze
January 26-27
Rice University intramural fields
Houston, TX

Benefits of this tournament include:
- 2 days of competitive Ultimate
- USA Ultimate Sanctioning
- Relatively warm weather (well, we can't promise that, but it IS Houston)
- Usual tournament amenities (bagels, Gatorade, etc.)
- Saturday night dinner event
- Certified athletic trainer
- Prizes for the winning team

This event is sanctioned by USA Ultimate- visit this link for more information regarding rostering your team for this event: http://www.usaultimate.org/resources/sanctioning/event_registration.aspx
